AE1 Program

Canon

The Canon AE-1 Program is a classic 35mm film camera that blends vintage charm with easy-to-use automatic modes, making it perfect for both beginners and serious photographers. With its sharp results, durable build, and iconic design, it’s a timeless way to capture photography the way it was meant to be.

C3 Professional

Mamiya

The Mamiya C3 Professional is a legendary medium format twin-lens reflex camera built for photographers who demand exceptional image quality and full creative control. With interchangeable lenses and razor-sharp optics, it delivers stunning detail and depth that make every shot feel truly professional.

K1000

Pentax

The Pentax K1000 is a legendary 35mm film camera known for its simplicity, durability, and perfect introduction to manual photography. Trusted by generations of students and professionals alike, it delivers consistently sharp results while teaching you the fundamentals of exposure control.

X7000

Minolta

The Minolta X-700 is a highly regarded 35mm film camera that combines full manual control with smooth, reliable automatic exposure modes. Loved by both beginners and advanced photographers, it delivers crisp, professional-quality images in a compact and easy-to-use body.

D Model

Yashica

The Yashica D is a classic medium format twin-lens reflex camera known for its solid build and beautifully sharp 6×6 images. With its simple controls and timeless design, it’s a reliable choice for photographers who want an authentic film shooting experience.

OM-1

Olympus

The Olympus OM-1 is a compact, lightweight 35mm SLR that delivers professional-level performance in a beautifully minimal design. Renowned for its quiet mechanical shutter and exceptional Zuiko lenses, it’s a timeless favorite for photographers who value precision and portability.
